# Onboarding Guide | வழிகாட்டி

The onboarding guide is the first interaction a new user has with i8e10, explaining the application's unique philosophy.

## Educational Content | கல்வி உள்ளடக்கம்
The guide covers three key pillars:
1. **Privacy**: Explaining that data is local and encrypted.
2. **Double-Entry**: Introducing the concept of accounts and balanced transactions.
3. **Forgiving Reconciliation**: Encouraging users to focus on the present rather than perfect past records.

## User Experience | பயனர் அனுபவம்
- **Step-by-Step**: A carousel-style guide that walks the user through the core value proposition.
- **Actionable**: Ends with the mandatory password and recovery phrase setup.
