Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Your privacy is important to us. i8·e10 is designed to protect your private finance management data by keeping it on your device, encrypted and under your control. It is a true offline expense tracker.

1. Local Data

  • All personal financial data you enter (transactions, debts, investments, etc.) is stored locally on your device.
  • This data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Anonymous Usage Data

  • We collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used, general app performance) to help us improve the App.
  • No IP addresses, device fingerprints, or personal identifiers are collected.
  • This data cannot be linked back to you or your financial information.

3. Data Retention

Anonymous usage data is kept only as long as it is useful for improving the App. Once it is no longer needed, it is deleted or aggregated in a way that cannot identify any usage patterns.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or anonymous data with third parties. If third-party tools are ever used to process anonymous data, they will receive only the minimum necessary and will not receive personal information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
