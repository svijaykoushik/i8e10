Privacy Policy

Your data is yours alone. We built i8·e10 with a "Zero-Knowledge" architecture.

Last Updated: February 2026

Summary

i8·e10 is an offline-first application. We do not have servers that store your financial data. Everything is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB) and encrypted using the Web Crypto API.

01. Data Sovereignty

All private finance management data you enter—expenses, income, debts, investments—resides strictly on your device. Unlike other cloud-based apps, we cannot see, sell, or analyze your financial habits because we simply do not have access to your data.

02. Encryption

We use the Web Crypto API to encrypt your critical data at rest. Your password creates a cryptographic key that is used to lock and unlock your data. Important: Since we don't not store your password, we cannot recover it for you. You are solely responsible for your password and recovery phrase.

03. Anonymous Analytics

To improve the application, we may collect minimal, anonymous usage data (e.g., "bulk import feature was used"). This telemetry contains absolutely no financial data, no personally identifiable information (PII), and no IP addresses that are linked to user profiles.

04. Third Parties

Since we do not collect personal data, we have nothing to share with third-party advertisers or data brokers. The app is ad-free and tracking-free regarding your personal identity.
